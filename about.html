About Us

Khopesh is an Indian indie game studio specializing in 2D-style games for computers and the internet.

Since our founding in 2024, we have successfully launched more than four games.

Our games prioritize enjoyable 2D gampeplay with a classic aesthetic, vibrant visuals, challenging puzzles that simulate the mind.

Our games are developed using the Godot game engine.
