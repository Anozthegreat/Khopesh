About


Khopesh is an one man game studio specializing in 2D-style games for computers and the internet. Since its founding in 2024, I have successfully launched more than four games. My games prioritize enjoyable 2D gameplay with a classic aesthetic, vibrant visuals, and challenging puzzles.


Khopesh is dedicated to bring back the nostalgic experience of classic games, offering a space where gamers can relive their favorite moments and discover timeless classics.


With a passion for pixel art and old-school mechanics, Khopesh delivers an authentic gaming experience while embracing modern enhancements.


My games are developed using the Godot and Renpy game engines.
